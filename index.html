The Study Event

Our server hosts a study event every alternative week wherein the participants earn special rewards like cool colour roles and badges.

What is the study event?

An event where we compete against each other by studying to get the top spot in the leaderboard of the server. We join the silent (muted) voice call groups (You can also share your screen if you want) to stay connected with everybody and also to track our Study time which is used to determine the ranks in the leaderboard.

How Does it work?

As long as you are in a voice channel, your study time will be tracked automatically and your rank will be updated in the leaderboard.

Is it ok to stay in VC while on a break?

5-10 minutes of breaks between your study sessions are acceptable. There is no need to leave and rejoin the VC.

Members are requested not to cheat in the Study Event by simply staying in the voice channel and not actually studying. If you are taking a break for more than 10 minutes, please leave the voice channel so that your long break times won't be accidentally counted in your Study time and please try not to chat for too long while you are in a session.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here we answer some of your most asked questions.

  • How does this server work?

    A community that aims to help each other with our academic troubles, this server is a place where you can study with other people. We have students from various fields, all of them helpful and kind, endeavouring to achieve their own goals. We aspire to provide a safe space for students where they can study in a library-esque atmosphere, as it definitely helps to have some company while studying knowing that you aren't alone. It helps with accountability and the leaderboard provides healthy competition, giving you an incentive to work hard.

  • What is the Study Event?

    An event where we compete against each other by studying to get the top spot in the leaderboard of the server. We join the silent (muted) voice call groups (You can also screenshare if you want) to stay connected with everyone and also to track our Study time which is used to determine the leaderboard of the Study Events. It's simply comforting to know you are not alone and there are other people working alongside you!

  • Can I get my doubts cleared?

    Study Adda has an interactive community that is always ready to help you. You can be assured that your academic queries/doubts will be cleared as long as you are respectful.

  • Who is this server for?

    Our server is accepting to all students. You can be a university or school student, or may even be self-studying! It doesn't matter provided that you are working towards an academic goal.
