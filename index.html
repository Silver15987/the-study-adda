The Study Event

Our server hosts a study event every alternative week. Where in exchange for studying, the participants earn special rewards like cool color roles and badges.

What is the study event?

A event where we compete against each other by studying to get the top spot in the leaderboard of the server. We join the silent (muted) voice call groups (You can also screenshare if you want) to stay connected with everyone and also to track our Study time which is used to determine the leaderboard of the Study Events. It's simply comforting to know you are not alone and there are other people working alongside you!

How Does it work?

As long as you are in voice channel, your study time will be counted automatically and your rank will be updated in the leaderboard

Is it ok to stay in VC while on a break?

5-10 mins of breaks between your study sessions are ok. There is no need to leave and rejoin the vc.

Members are requested not to cheat in Study Event by simply staying in the voice channel and not actually studying. If you are taking a break of more than 10 minutes, please leave the voice channel so that your long break times won't be accidentally counted in your Study time and please try not to chat for too long while you are in a session
